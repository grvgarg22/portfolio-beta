who i am!

Hi, I'm Gaurav Garg, a passionate UI/UX Designer from India with extensive experience designing and improving UI projects across various industries, including Telecom & Print Media, Ed-Tech, Real Estate, Healthcare, Sports, and Fin-Tech.

I excel at problem-solving and leverage my skills in tools like Figma, Adobe XD, and Adobe Photoshop to create designs that strike the perfect balance between usability and aesthetics. While I stay up-to-date with the latest design trends, I also understand the value of using traditional design elements when required.

I'm committed to making UI simple, easy to use, and elegant in appearance. Furthermore, I specialize in providing clean, easy-to-understand, and reusable UI codes in HTML5, CSS3, SASS, jQuery, and frameworks like Bootstrap and Tailwind.

I also have expertise in website optimization, ensuring that your site performs at its best. So, whether you need help with a new project or improving an existing one, don't hesitate to contact me. I'll be more than happy to assist you to the best of my capabilities.
